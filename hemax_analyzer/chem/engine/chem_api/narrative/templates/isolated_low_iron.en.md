# Low serum iron
icon: 🩸
severity: low
---
Your serum iron is low. This can be an **early stage of deficiency**, but alone it's not enough for diagnosis — iron levels fluctuate during the day.

Ferritin is a better marker of iron stores. If not measured, it's worth getting.
---
actions:
- Get **ferritin**, TIBC, TSAT — for the full picture
- CBC to check for anemia
- If ferritin is also low, that confirms iron deficiency
