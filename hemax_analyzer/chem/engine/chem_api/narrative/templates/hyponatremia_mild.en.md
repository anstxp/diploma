# Low sodium
icon: ⚡
severity: medium
---
Your sodium ({{ sodium }} mmol/L) is below normal. Mild hyponatremia is often asymptomatic but the cause needs to be identified.

Possible causes:
- **SIADH** — most common, especially in elderly
- **Diuretic use** (especially thiazides)
- **Heart or liver failure**
- **Excessive water intake**
- **Hypothyroidism or adrenal insufficiency**
---
actions:
- See a GP
- Get: repeat sodium, serum osmolality, urine osmolality, TSH, cortisol
- Review medications (especially diuretics, antidepressants)
- Don't overdo water — drink to thirst
---
red_flags:
- Severe headache, nausea, vomiting
- Confusion
- Muscle cramps
