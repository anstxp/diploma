# Metabolic alkalosis
icon: ⚡
severity: low
---
Your bicarbonate ({{ bicarbonate }} mmol/L) is elevated. This signals **metabolic alkalosis** — blood alkalinization.

Common causes:
- **Prolonged vomiting** — loss of stomach acid
- **Diuretic use** (especially loop, thiazides)
- **Hypokalemia**
- Excessive antacid (bicarbonate) intake
- Hyperaldosteronism
---
actions:
- See your GP
- Get: repeat bicarbonate, potassium, chloride
- If on diuretics, discuss with your doctor
- Drink adequate water
- If vomiting, see a doctor for cause workup
