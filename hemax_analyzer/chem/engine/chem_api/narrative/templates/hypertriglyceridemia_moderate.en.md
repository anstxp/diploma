# Elevated triglycerides
icon: 💗
severity: low
---
Your triglycerides ({{ trigly }} mg/dL) are above normal but not critically high. Common causes: excess simple carbs and alcohol, obesity, sedentary lifestyle, uncontrolled diabetes.

At moderate elevations, lifestyle changes typically produce significant improvement.
---
actions:
- Repeat fasting (≥12 hours) — eating significantly raises triglycerides
- Cut simple carbs: sugar, sweets, sweetened drinks, fruit juices, white flour
- Limit alcohol (especially beer)
- Regular aerobic activity (30+ min/day)
- Additional tests: HbA1c, TSH
- Repeat lipids in 3 months
