# Acute pancreatitis pattern
icon: 🔴
severity: high
---
Your {% if lipase %}lipase ({{ lipase }} U/L){% else %}amylase ({{ amylase }} U/L){% endif %} is **markedly elevated**. Such values are characteristic of **acute pancreatitis** — acute pancreatic inflammation.

Acute pancreatitis is an emergency requiring immediate medical care. Most common causes: gallstones, heavy alcohol use, high triglycerides.
---
actions:
- **Go to the ER immediately**, even if pain is mild
- Don't eat or drink alcohol before evaluation
- Bring your medication list
- Likely need ultrasound, abdominal CT, hospital admission
- If triglycerides are also high, this can be the cause
---
red_flags:
- Severe upper abdominal pain radiating to back
- Pain worsens after eating
- Nausea, repeated vomiting
- Fever
- Jaundice
