# Mixed liver injury (hepatocellular + cholestatic)
icon: 🟠
severity: medium
---
You have simultaneous elevation of **ALT/AST** (cell-breakdown enzymes) and **ALP/GGT** (cholestasis markers). This **mixed pattern** can indicate:

- Severe toxic (e.g. alcoholic) liver injury
- Acute viral hepatitis in the cholestatic phase
- Drug-induced hepatotoxicity
- Autoimmune hepatitis with cholangitis
---
actions:
- **See a gastroenterologist or hepatologist within 1-2 weeks**
- Get: HBsAg, anti-HCV, anti-HAV, autoantibodies (ANA, AMA), repeat full liver panel, liver ultrasound
- **Complete alcohol abstinence** pending workup
- Review every medication and supplement with your doctor
- If symptomatic, don't delay
---
red_flags:
- Jaundice — **see a doctor today**
- Confusion, drowsiness — go to ER (possible hepatic encephalopathy)
- Blood in vomit or dark tarry stools
- Fever
