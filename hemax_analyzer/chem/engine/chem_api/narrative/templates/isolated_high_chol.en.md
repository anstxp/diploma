# Mildly elevated cholesterol
icon: 💗
severity: low
---
Your total cholesterol or non-HDL cholesterol is slightly above optimal. Not a critical elevation, but a reason to monitor cardiovascular risk more carefully.
---
actions:
- Get LDL specifically (if not done) — the most prognostic marker
- Lifestyle: Mediterranean diet, regular activity
- See a doctor if other risk factors (BP↑, smoking, family history)
- Repeat lipids in 6 months
