# Mildly elevated CK
icon: 💪
severity: low
---
Your CK ({{ ck }} U/L) is mildly elevated. CK is a muscle enzyme; levels rise after:

- Intense exercise, especially strength training (1-2 days after)
- Prolonged physical activity
- Intramuscular injections
- Statin use (sometimes)
- Hypothyroidism (less common)

Mild elevation without symptoms is usually not concerning.
---
actions:
- If recent training, repeat in 5-7 days of rest
- Get: TSH — to rule out hypothyroidism
- If on statins with muscle pain, see your doctor
- Drink plenty of water
---
red_flags:
- Severe muscle pain
- Dark urine
- Weakness
- Values >1000 — repeat soon
