# Elevated bilirubin
icon: 🟡
severity: low
---
Your total bilirubin ({{ bilirubin_total }} mg/dL) is elevated. Without fractionated bilirubin and other liver tests, it's hard to determine the exact cause.

Possibilities: Gilbert's syndrome, hemolysis (increased red cell breakdown), bile flow obstruction.
---
actions:
- Get fractionated bilirubin (direct + indirect)
- Get a full liver panel (ALT, AST, ALP, GGT) if not already done
- See your GP with these results
