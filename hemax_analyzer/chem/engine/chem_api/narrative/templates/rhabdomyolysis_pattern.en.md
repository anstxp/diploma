# Very high CK — possible muscle breakdown
icon: 🔴
severity: high
---
Your CK ({{ ck }} U/L) is **very high**. Values above 1500 U/L can indicate **rhabdomyolysis** — massive muscle breakdown that can damage kidneys.

Most common causes: heavy exercise (especially with dehydration), trauma, prolonged immobility, statin use, certain infections, post-seizure injury.
---
actions:
- **Go to the ER today**
- Drink plenty of water — this helps protect kidneys
- Get: creatinine, potassium, urine myoglobin
- If on statins, inform your doctor (changes may be needed)
- If after heavy exercise, stop training until evaluated
---
red_flags:
- Dark "tea-colored" or "cola-colored" urine
- Severe muscle pain and weakness
- Reduced urine output
- Palpitations, irregular heartbeat
- Confusion
