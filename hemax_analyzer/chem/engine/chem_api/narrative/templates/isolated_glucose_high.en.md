# Glucose slightly above normal
icon: 🩸
severity: low
---
Your fasting glucose ({{ glucose }} mg/dL) is slightly above the upper limit, but HbA1c is not measured or is normal. This could be:

- **Stress hyperglycemia** — a one-off elevation during stress or illness
- **Non-fasting** — if you ate less than 8 hours before the test
- **Early-stage** impaired glucose tolerance

Without HbA1c it's hard to say precisely — broader context is needed.
---
actions:
- **Get HbA1c** — the most important marker of glucose metabolism
- Repeat fasting glucose after a week (≥8 hour fast)
- If both elevated, see a doctor for OGTT
- Review diet and activity
