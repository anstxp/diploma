# Low HDL ("good" cholesterol)
icon: 💗
severity: low
---
Your HDL ({{ hdl }} mg/dL) is below desirable levels. HDL is the "good" cholesterol that helps remove excess cholesterol from artery walls. Low HDL is a cardiovascular risk factor, but **medications rarely raise it effectively**.

What works: physical activity, weight loss, smoking cessation.
---
actions:
- 150 min/week of moderate activity — most effective way to raise HDL
- If you smoke, quitting can raise HDL by 5-10%
- Moderate intake of nuts, olive oil, fish (omega-3)
- If overweight, even 5% weight loss improves HDL
- Check other risk factors with your doctor (BP, glucose, family history)
