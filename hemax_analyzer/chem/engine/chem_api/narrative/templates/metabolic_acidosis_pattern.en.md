# Metabolic acidosis
icon: ⚡
severity: medium
---
Your bicarbonate ({{ bicarbonate }} mmol/L) is low. This signals **metabolic acidosis** — blood acidification, most often from kidneys or acid accumulation.

Possible causes:
- **CKD** — kidneys can't excrete acids well
- **Diabetic ketoacidosis** — in uncontrolled diabetes
- **Lactic acidosis** — in severe infections, shock
- **Bicarbonate loss from diarrhea**
- Certain medications, toxins
---
actions:
- See a doctor — the cause needs identification
- Get: repeat bicarbonate, creatinine, eGFR, glucose, anion gap
- If diabetic, check ketones
- Review medications
---
red_flags:
- Rapid or deep breathing
- Nausea, vomiting, abdominal pain
- Confusion, drowsiness
- Fruity breath
- Diabetes + acidosis + symptoms — **go to ER** (possible ketoacidosis)
