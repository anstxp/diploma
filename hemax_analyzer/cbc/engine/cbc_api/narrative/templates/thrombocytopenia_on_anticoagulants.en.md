# Thrombocytopenia + anticoagulants = bleeding risk
icon: 🚨
severity: high
---
Low platelets combined with anticoagulant therapy significantly elevates bleeding risk.
---
actions:
- Contact prescribing physician today
- Do not stop anticoagulants on your own
---
red_flags:
- Bleeding from gums, nose, mucosa
- Black tarry stool
- Sudden severe headache
