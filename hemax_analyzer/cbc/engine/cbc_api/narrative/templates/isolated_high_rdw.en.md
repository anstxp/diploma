# Uneven red-cell size
icon: ℹ️
severity: low
---
RDW ({{ rdw }}%) measures how different your red cells are from each other in size. Elevation often reflects the bone marrow producing cells of mixed sizes — frequently an early marker of nutrient deficiency (iron, B12, folate) before overt anaemia appears.
---
actions:
- Check **ferritin, B12, folate**
- If normal, monitor at next visit
