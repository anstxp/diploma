# See a doctor right away — all three blood cell lines are low
icon: 🔴
severity: high
---
Your results show all three main types of blood cells are reduced at the same time — **white cells** (infection defence), **haemoglobin/red cells** (oxygen delivery), and **platelets** (clotting). This is called **pancytopenia** and always needs a prompt medical evaluation.

Causes range from relatively benign (side effects of certain medications, viral infections) to serious bone-marrow conditions. It's not possible to tell which from this test alone — further work-up is needed.
---
actions:
- See a GP or primary-care doctor **within the next 24-48 hours**
- If that's not possible, go to an emergency department
- Bring a printout of this result and a list of all medications you take
- Avoid situations with risk of cuts or trauma while clotting is impaired
---
red_flags:
- High fever, chills, or signs of infection — go to ER now
- Bleeding gums, nosebleeds, unexplained bruising — go to ER now
- Severe fatigue, shortness of breath at rest, dizziness — go to ER now
