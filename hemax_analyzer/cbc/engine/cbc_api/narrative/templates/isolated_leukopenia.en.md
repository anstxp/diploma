# Low white cell count
icon: ⚪
severity: low
---
Your total white cell count ({{ wbc }} ×10⁹/L) is below normal. Causes vary: recent viral infection, B12/folate deficiency, some medications, sometimes **simply individual variation** (some people have a naturally lower baseline).
---
actions:
- If you feel well, repeat the test in 3-4 weeks
- Check B12 and folate if this is a persistent finding
- If you recently had a cold or flu, this is normal
---
red_flags:
- Recurrent infections, prolonged infections that don't heal
- Unexplained fever
