# Anemia with diabetes
icon: 🩸
severity: medium
---
You have **diabetes** and now low hemoglobin. This may be **diabetic nephropathy** — kidney damage from long-term diabetes. Kidneys produce erythropoietin (a hormone that stimulates RBC production); nephropathy reduces it, causing anemia.
---
actions:
- Check kidney function: creatinine, eGFR, urine microalbumin
- See an endocrinologist or nephrologist
- Test ferritin and transferrin saturation
- Do not start iron supplements without testing first
---
red_flags:
- Leg or face swelling
- Decreased urine output
- Severe weakness or shortness of breath
