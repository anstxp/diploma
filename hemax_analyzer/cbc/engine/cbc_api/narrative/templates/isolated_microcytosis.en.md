# Smaller-than-normal red cells without anaemia
icon: ℹ️
severity: low
---
Your red cells are smaller than normal (MCV = {{ mcv }} fL) but your haemoglobin is normal. This may be:

- **Early-stage iron deficiency** — before anaemia has developed
- **Thalassemia trait** — an inherited trait
- Chronic inflammation
---
actions:
- Get **ferritin, iron, transferrin** — key iron-store markers
- If ferritin is normal, discuss haemoglobin electrophoresis with your doctor
- Don't take iron blindly
