# Elevated eosinophils
icon: 🌿
severity: low
---
Your eosinophil count is elevated. Eosinophils are white cells that activate in:

- **Allergic conditions** (asthma, allergic rhinitis, food/drug allergies)
- **Parasitic infections** (especially in recent travellers to tropical areas)
- Some skin conditions (eczema, psoriasis)
- Less commonly — medication reactions or autoimmune disease
---
actions:
- If you have known allergies or asthma — this is likely a manifestation (no special action)
- If you recently travelled to tropics or Asia — get a **stool test for parasite eggs**
- See a GP, especially if you have skin rash, itching, or frequent infections
- Muscle pain or weakness is a reason to definitely see a doctor
---
red_flags:
- Intense all-body itching
- Rapidly spreading rash
- Muscle pain, weakness
