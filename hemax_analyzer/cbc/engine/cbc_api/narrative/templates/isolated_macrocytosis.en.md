# Larger-than-normal red cells without anaemia
icon: ℹ️
severity: low
---
Your red cells are larger than normal (MCV = {{ mcv }} fL) but your haemoglobin is normal. Causes:

- **Early B12 or folate deficiency**
- Regular alcohol use
- Hypothyroidism
- Certain medications
---
actions:
- Get **B12, folate, TSH** tested
- If you drink regularly, discuss with a doctor
- Review your medication list
