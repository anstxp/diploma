# Elevated neutrophil-to-lymphocyte ratio
icon: ℹ️
severity: low
---
Your NLR is elevated. This is a marker of systemic inflammation — even when neutrophil and lymphocyte counts individually are normal. Most often reflects recent or mild infection, stress, or a chronic inflammatory process.
---
actions:
- If you feel well, no special action needed
- If this is a repeat finding or you have chronic conditions, discuss with a GP
