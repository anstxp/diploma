# Mildly elevated white cells
icon: ⚪
severity: low
---
Your total white cell count ({{ wbc }} ×10⁹/L) is slightly above normal, but the breakdown (neutrophils, lymphocytes) doesn't show a clear infection pattern. This can occur with:

- Recent infection (recovery phase)
- Stress or intense physical exertion
- Some medications (corticosteroids)
- Smoking
---
actions:
- If no symptoms — repeat the test in 2-4 weeks
- If it persists, see a doctor
- Before retesting, avoid intense exercise and stress
---
red_flags:
- Onset of fever, fatigue, pain
- Enlarged lymph nodes
